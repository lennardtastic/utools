About this Generator

This is the extended converter for generating combicodes. This combi code includes the setting of various value like the custom variables, z-index, and even the setEventCallback. Just check the boxes and use the drop-down to create a combicode of your choice. Finish by clicking the "Generate Combicode" button and download or copy it before sending it to the customer.

Usage of the combicode generator is at your own risk. Always make sure to double check if everything is working as expected before sending it to the customer!
